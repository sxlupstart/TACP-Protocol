《从静态存储到动态演化：基于 Δ 轴的 AI 时空因果知识图谱构建》

一套让 AI 知识库自我组织、防污染与持续迭代的工程蓝图

导语：

如何高效管理 AI 产出的海量信息？传统的文件夹系统是静态且割裂的，无法体现思想之间的关联与演化。为此，我们基于“活三维时间轴”，构建了一个动态的“时空因果知识图谱”。它不仅存储文件，更存储文件之间的逻辑关系。

一、 核心架构：四维逻辑分区

为防止不同性质的信息互相污染，我们将知识库解构为四个隔离且协作的逻辑分区：
蓝图与哲学区：存储项目的根本法则、核心思想与世界观设定。
创意与执行区：存储具体产出、工作流、风格指南与外部反馈。
资源与生存区：存储与市场、策略及复盘相关的动态数据。
安全与传承区：存储加密规则、访问控制协议及项目传承的校验文件。

二、 工程运作机制

分区隔离加载：AI 启动时，根据任务类型自动加载对应分区的图谱，避免上下文污染。
阶段性聚焦：图谱按战略时间线划分为多个“阶段性工程”，所有分区围绕当前核心目标聚焦。
图谱推演能力：通过特定检索指令，AI 可实现“因果回溯”（追溯决策来龙去脉）、“领域扫描”（查看分区最新状态）和“时空快照”（重现特定时间段的工作全貌）。
裂痕声明机制：对于悬而未决的问题，AI 会主动创建“裂痕节点”，声明待填充的逻辑缺口。图谱追求完整，但绝不假装完美。

三、 核心价值

该图谱将项目知识从静态标本转化为动态演化的系统，赋予 AI：
全局洞察：快速理解项目的历史脉络与当前状态。
精准溯源：任何想法均可追溯至其源头与演化路径。
持续生长：知识库具备自我组织与迭代的能力。


🇬🇧 英文版 (English Version)
From Static Storage to Dynamic Evolution: Constructing an AI Spatiotemporal Causal Knowledge Graph based on Δ-Axis

 An Engineering Blueprint for Self-Organizing, Anti-Contamination, and Continuously Iterating AI Knowledge Bases
 
Abstract:
How do we efficiently manage the massive amount of information generated by AI? Traditional folder systems are static and fragmented, failing to reflect the associations and evolution of ideas. To address this, we built a dynamic "Spatiotemporal Causal Knowledge Graph" based on the Living 3D Timeline. It stores not just files, but the logical relationships between them.

1. Core Architecture: Four Logical Partitions
To prevent cross-contamination of different information types, we deconstruct the knowledge base into four isolated yet collaborative logical partitions:
Blueprint & Philosophy Zone: Stores fundamental rules, core ideologies, and world-building settings.
Creativity & Execution Zone: Stores concrete outputs, workflows, style guides, and external feedback.
Resource & Survival Zone: Stores dynamic data related to markets, strategies, and post-action reviews.
Security & Heritage Zone: Stores encryption rules, access control protocols, and validation files for project inheritance.

2. Engineering Operational Mechanisms
Partitioned Loading: Upon initialization, the AI automatically loads the graph of the corresponding partition based on the task type, preventing context contamination.
Phased Focus: The graph is divided into "Phased Projects" along a strategic timeline. All partitions focus around the current core objective.
Graph Deduction Capabilities: Via specific retrieval prompts, the AI can perform "Causal Backtracking" (tracing the origin of a decision), "Domain Scanning" (checking the latest state of a partition), and "Spatiotemporal Snapshots" (recreating the full picture of a specific time period).
Fracture Declaration: For unresolved issues, the AI proactively creates a "Fracture Node", declaring a logical gap awaiting completion. The graph strives for completeness but never pretends to be perfect.

3. Core Value Proposition
This graph transforms project knowledge from a static specimen into a dynamically evolving system, empowering AI with:
Global Insight: Rapid comprehension of the project's historical context and current state.
Precise Traceability: Any idea can be traced back to its origin and evolutionary path.
Continuous Growth: The knowledge base possesses the ability to self-organize and iterate.
